Personal Profile


Kia ora everyone,

My name is Jerry (Xiaochuan) Gao. Originally, I am from China and moved to New Zealand four years ago. I love travelling and sports. I have lived and studied in many counties, Korea, Australia, Japan and America for a couple of years. My hobbies are gym, badminton and volleyball.

I am a fully registered teacher with New Zealand Teaching Council. As a professional teacher, I am passionate about working and playing with children. On my teaching practice, I would love to take children outside to do some exercises, ball games, and group activities. I am patient and always listen to children so that their needs are met. As an Asian teacher, I value every culture in the world. Although I am impacted by the Asian traditional values, I value multiple cultures. I respect the diverse world and am trying to be an ‘international’ teacher. I speak fluent English, Mandarin, and Korean. In addition, I know a little Russian and Maori.

Speaking of my education background, I completed my BA in literacy in 2012 in China, Master in interpretation in 2015 in America and South Korean and Graduate diploma in Teaching 2018 in Aotearoa.
